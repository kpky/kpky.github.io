Insurance Coverage and Bad Faith

The Firm conducts a full array of insurance coverage litigation and counseling including both casualty and property claims, including automobile, homeowners, commercial general liability, excess/umbrella, employment practices and other insurance policies.

The Firm also defends against insurers for bad faith, and conducts investigations and defends cases involving arson, fraud, material misrepresentation and other insured misconduct.
