    I designed this iPhone app in conjunction with the 2016 GROK Event site. I wanted it to have the same look and feel as the website, so therefore utilized the same colors, as well as the same basic structure and rhythm.

    One of the requirements of this iPhone app was that it had to provide live and up to date information for the attendees. To implement this, I decided to incorporate a countdown to the next speaker/activity. You can see the countdown displayed in the top right hand corner of each view.

    If you want to skip ahead or view events from a past day, you can do so by clicking on any one of the days on the Schedule page. Likewise, if you want to find information about a specific person, the People page is alphabetized for attendees to quicky find what they need.

    I also created a clickable prototype of the app in InVision, which brings the app to life and lets you experience how it would function on your iPhone.
