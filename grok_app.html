GROK Event, iPhone App

I designed this iPhone app in conjunction with the 2016 GROK Event site. I wanted it to have the same look and feel as the site, so therefore utilized the same colors, basic structure, and rhythm.

One of the requirements of this iPhone app was that it had to provide live and up to date information for the attendees. To implement this, I decided to incorporate a countdown to the next speaker or activity. You can see this in the top right hand corner of each view, a countdown to Jenn Lukas, who is scheduled to give her Keynote Presentation in 35 minutes.

If you want to skip ahead or view events from a past day, you can do so by clicking on any one of the days on the Schedule page. Likewise, if you want to find information about a specific person, the People page is alphabetized for attendees to quicky find speaker info.

I also created a clickable prototype of the app in InVision, which lets you experience how it would function on your iPhone.
