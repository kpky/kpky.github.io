I am a recent graduate of the Iron Yard Academy, Columbia User Interface Design Program. During this 12 week intensive program, we explored the entire production process for building responsive interfaces and designing for multiple devices -- including discovery and ideation, the design process (thumbnails, wireframes, and Photoshop mockups) HTML, CSS & SASS, basic Javascipt & JQuery, Github, the Git workflow, and asset management.

Born in Nashville, Tennessee and raised in Miami, Florida and Charlotte, North Carolina, I the University of North Carolina, School of the Arts in Winston-Salem for high school, and then went on to study at Tufts University and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, where I earned a BFA in Studio Arts in 2006. After graduating, I spent 3 years working in the commercial post-production field in Boston and then relocated to Los Angeles to work in the film industry. I stayed out west for two years and then relocated to Charlotte in 2011, where I joined Apple Retail. I currently work in Inventory at the Southpark location.
