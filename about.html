    I have a passion for designing responsive interfaces that are clean, intuitive, and smart. I enjoy the challenge of designing interfaces that function seamlessly and look incredible on every type of device and screen size.

    Art and design have always been woven in the fabric of what I do. My art education started in high school at the prestigious North Carolina School of the Arts Visual Arts/Design program and then continued at the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston and Tufts University where I earned my BFA in Studio Arts in 2006.

    I recently completed a User Interface Design program during which I explored the entire responsive design process from discovery and ideation, UX research, wireframes and mockups, to the development with HTML5 and CSS3. Besides designing and coding, I also utilyze prototyping tools to bring my designs to life.

    I have 10 years of experience in fast paced creative environments in Boston, Los Angeles, and Charlotte. I have a solid understanding of design principles and typography, an eye for detail, and a love of coding. I am seeking a full time position with a company or agency that will allow me to grow and learn in the areas of User Interface Design and Responsive Design.
