I am a recent graduate of the User Interface Design Program at the Iron Yard Academy, Columbia. During this 12-week intensive program, we learned how to build and design responsive web interfaces. We explored the entire production process -- from discovery and ideation and the design process (thumbnails, wireframes, Photoshop mockups), to the development (HTML, CSS & SASS, basic Javascipt & JQuery), Github and the Git workflow, and asset management. We also used programs such as InVision to create and test prototypes.

Art and design have always been in the fabric of what I do. Born in Nashville, Tennessee and raised in Miami, Florida and Charlotte, North Carolina, I attended the University of North Carolina, School of the Arts in Winston-Salem for high school, and then went on to study at Tufts University and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, where I earned a BFA in Studio Arts in 2006.

After graduating, I spent 3 years working in commercial post-production in Boston and then relocated to Los Angeles in 2009, after getting a job on a film set in western North Carolina. While in Los Angeles, I worked on various types of productions and had many jobs on set, but found my niche in the art department, doing set decoration and working with props. I returned to Charlotte in 2011, where I joined Apple Retail.

I'm seeking a full time position that will allow me to build my knowledge of responsive web design and development and connect with other professionals to share ideas and best practices.
