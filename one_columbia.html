    The One Columbia 'My Passport' App was designed to encourage the residents of Columbia, SC to attend more art and cultural events in the community. Attendees locate the icon at the event (usually on a poster at the event) and use their iPhone camera to snap a photo of it. The geo-location feature incorporated into the app confirms their location. Attendees can also check-in using a unique hashtag if their iPhone Camera is disabled.

    I also created a clickable prototype of the app in InVision, which allows you to experience how it would function on your iPhone.
