One Columbia, My Passport, iPhone App

This iPhone app was designed to encourage the residents of Columbia, SC to attend more events in the community. Icons were designed for each category of event (Dance, Literature, Music, Visual Arts, Multi Cultural, Theater, and Design/Public Art), and are posted at each destination. The attendees use their iPhone camera to snap a photo of the icon, and the Geo location incorporated into the app confirms their location. Attendees can also check in using a hashtag, if their iPhone Camera is disabled.

I also created a clickable prototype of the app in InVision, which lets you experience how it would function on your iPhone.
